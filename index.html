Professional Philosophy

The journey throughout a career, whether in a single profession or multiple, relies on finding your own way. It is not necessary to search out your individual path alone, though. Advice from others and collaboration provide guidance along the way.

Throughout my journey I have discovered that I am an innovative workhorse who enjoys serving others and accomplishing goals. I like to disassemble a problem piece by piece, and then find the most efficient way to assemble the solution. I am grounded in trial and error because it provides a deep understanding for any given problem.

I enjoy the "behind the scenes" aspect of operations. I am passionate about learning, in professional and personal endeavours. Every day that I learn or experience something new, I am joyful and grateful.
